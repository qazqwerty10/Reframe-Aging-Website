Areas of Care

Reframe Aging focuses on healthy aging, physical capacity, balance, fall prevention, vestibular rehabilitation, osteoporosis-informed strength training, back pain and sciatica care, post-surgical rehabilitation, mobility, gait, and the Annual Capacity Assessment.

Serving Denver

Based in Denver, Colorado, Reframe Aging supports adults across the Denver area who want to stay strong, mobile, independent, and engaged in life. Led by Tim Nguyen, PT, DPT, GCS, a board-certified Geriatric Clinical Specialist.
