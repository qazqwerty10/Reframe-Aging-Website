Vision

A future where aging means capability, purpose, and independence.

We envision a future where growing older is not defined by decline or dependency, but by the ability to remain active, connected, and engaged in the people, places, and pursuits that give life meaning.

Mission

Helping adults preserve and expand physical capacity.

Through evidence-based physical therapy, movement assessment, strength development, education, and proactive health strategies, Reframe Aging helps individuals maintain mobility, confidence, resilience, and independence across the lifespan.

Our philosophy

The Capacity Principle

Many people do not lose independence simply because they get older. They lose independence when the physical demands of life exceed their physical capacity. Our work focuses on building the reserve needed to keep life expansive.

Founder

Meet Tim Nguyen, PT, DPT, GCS

Tim Nguyen is a Doctor of Physical Therapy and board-certified Geriatric Clinical Specialist dedicated to helping adults maintain the physical capacity necessary for lifelong independence.

His clinical practice combines orthopedic rehabilitation, healthy aging principles, strength development, balance training, and preventative care to help individuals move confidently through every stage of life.

Tim founded Reframe Aging after recognizing that many physical limitations commonly attributed to aging are often influenced by factors that can be addressed through evidence-based intervention.

Outside of clinical practice, Tim mentors physical therapy students, develops community education programs, and continues advanced training in orthopedics, geriatrics, vestibular rehabilitation, and emerging healthcare technologies.
