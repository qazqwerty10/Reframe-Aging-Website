Who I Work With

Built for people
who think ahead.

Reframe Aging is not a traditional rehabilitation clinic. It is a practice for people who are not content to wait for a problem before addressing it — and for those recovering from one who want to come out stronger than before.

The proactive adult

You want to stay ahead of decline.

You are active and functional. You are not injured — you are investing. You want a clinician who can assess where you are, identify where you are vulnerable, and help you build the physical reserve you will need in the decades ahead. You are interested in regenerative medicine or sustainable habits, you are thinking seriously about healthspan, not just lifespan.

Recovering with purpose

You want more than getting back to baseline.

You have been through an injury, surgery, or significant health event. You are recovering — but you are thinking past recovery. You want to come out stronger, more resilient, and better prepared for the years ahead than you were before. Returning to where you were is the floor, not the ceiling.

Navigating change

Something has shifted and you want answers.

You have noticed changes in your balance, your strength, or your stamina. You are not sure if what you are experiencing is a normal part of aging or something that can be meaningfully addressed. You want honest assessment, clear communication, and a path forward — not just reassurance.

The active older adult

You plan to keep moving and want support doing it well.

You hike, swim, cycle, lift, play tennis, chase grandchildren, or simply move through life at a pace that matters to you. You want a clinician who takes your goals seriously regardless of your age, who understands the physical demands of an active life at 60 or 70 or 80, and who can help you sustain it.

Concerned about falls

You or someone you love wants to address fall risk directly.

Falls are not inevitable, and fear of falling is itself a significant risk factor — it changes behavior, reduces activity, and accelerates exactly the decline it is trying to avoid. Reframe Aging addresses fall risk through comprehensive balance assessment, vestibular evaluation, strength training, and building the confidence that comes from being genuinely capable.

Clinicians & students

You want to practice geriatric PT differently.

Reframe Aging is also built as a platform for clinicians and students interested in the Capacity Principle framework, longevity-informed physical therapy practice, and the continuum of care from recovery through prevention and longevity. Tim actively mentors and welcomes conversation with early-career clinicians and students.

What this is not

A note on fit.

Reframe Aging is not the right fit for every situation. If you need urgent surgical evaluation, specialized oncology care, or a high-volume insurance-based clinic with quick turnaround, there are excellent practices in Denver that will serve you better.

What Reframe Aging offers is depth, time, and a long-horizon clinical perspective. Sessions are deliberate. Assessment is thorough. The work is oriented toward where you want to be in ten years, not just how you feel after six visits.

If that is what you are looking for, reach out. Tim responds to every message personally and is happy to talk through whether this practice is a good fit before any commitment is made.
